About

Windows Master is primarily a window management application. It is certainly not the first of its kind. A few other notable window managing applications include: Display Fusion, WinSplit, and WindowPad.

All three applications have notable features. WinSplit pioneered the idea of sequencing. Display Fusion takes an aggressive approach of putting many options in the hands of the user, the most noteworthy feature possibly being orderly window movement. Finally, WindowPad shares some of those same features, but it is developer-friendly instead of user-friendly.

Windows Master is an augmentation of all three applications. The most noteworthy feature is the utilization of the Leap(™) Motion Controller. This brings an immersive experience where you may control your computer by simply a wave of your hand. Other notable features include: utilization of sequencing, orderly window movement, window snapping (or anchoring), and also a handy quick menu to give instant access to numerous window functions.

Getting Started

There's a key principle used throughout Windows Master that should be understood. Namely, each action will be exacted upon the currently active window -- that means the one window which is currently focused. Additionally, each action is monitor-sensitive. For example, Snap to Center will center the active window within the monitor the window is currently on. For more information on multi-monitor support. refer to the list below.
