About

Windows Master is primarily a window management application. It is certainly not the first of its kind. A few other notable window managing applications include: Display Fusion, WinSplit, and WindowPad.

All three applications have notable features. WinSplit pioneered the idea of sequencing. Display Fusion takes an aggressive approach of putting many options in the hands of the user, the most noteworthy feature possibly being ordered window movement. Finally, WindowPad shares some of those same features, but it is developer-friendly instead of user-friendly.

Windows Master is an augmentation of all three applications. The most noteworthy feature is the utilization of the Leap(™) Motion Controller. This brings the immersive experience where you may control your computer with simply a wave of your hand. Other notable features include: utilization of sequencing, ordered window movement, window snapping (or anchoring), and also a handy quick menu to give instant access to numerous window functions.

Every action in Windows Master may be mapped to a hotkey, and/or a Leap(™) Motion gesture. For more information on how to perform gestures, see Performing Gestures
