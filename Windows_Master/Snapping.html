Window Snapping

Window Snapping is a fast alternative to the "click-n-drag" mouse method of moving windows. It ensures maximum economy of workspace since it places windows at precise corners of the monitor.
Edit an action by double-clicking on a row. Alternativelty, highlight a row and then: press Alt + E, press F2, or press the icon.


Note: If you are unsure what an action does, take notice of the explanation text underneath the table.
